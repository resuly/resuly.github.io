About Me

A student from Monash University, majoring in Transportation Systems, also interested in ITS (Intelligent Transportation System), Web Development and Deep Learning. Most of the projects I have done are in the fields of ITS & Web Development as you can find this blog.

FYI, I am a member of Transport and Urban Planning Arena, and the co-founder of Empire Subtitle-making Group and V2TM.com.
